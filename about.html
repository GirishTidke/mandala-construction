Our Story

Founded on Passion,
Built on Trust

Mandala Design and Construction was founded in 1999 by a team of passionate civil engineers and architects who believed Nagpur deserved world-class construction at honest prices.

Over 25 years, we've grown from a small local firm to one of Nagpur's most respected construction companies - with over 150 completed projects ranging from luxury residences to large commercial complexes.

Our approach is simple: listen deeply, plan meticulously, build with the finest materials, and deliver on our promises. Every project carries our name and our reputation.
